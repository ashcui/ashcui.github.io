Online Ordering is a seamless digital ordering solution integrated with Toast POS and Toast has more than 10,000 live users around the world. To bring more revenue and user loyalty, Branded Online Ordering Pro is an upgrade to online ordering which offers the ability for a restaurant owner to build their own online ordering experience by customizing the brand color, fonts, etc.

White label app is a new beta product from the Digital Storefront Suite that allows Toast customers to launch their own branded online ordering apps.
