White Label App (Admin)
Online Ordering is a seamless online ordering solution integrated with Toast POS and Toast has more than 10,000 live users around the world.
To bring more revenue and user loyalty, Branded Online Ordering Pro is an upgrade to online ordering which offers the ability for a restaurant owner to build their own online ordering experience by customizing the brand color, fonts, etc.

This is a new beta product from the Digital Storefront Suite that allows Toast customers to launch their own white label apps.

*A white label app isÂ a generic application built by a company to resell it to another business that, in turn, can rebrand it as their own. This helps businesses have their own app with the least possible investment. White-labeling allows you to maintain your brand identity across client-facing applications.
