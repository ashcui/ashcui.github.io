With Google's expansion into San José, their vision is to work together with locals to imagine a part of downtown — one that draws from the City's culturally-rich history, vibrant personality, and innovative spirit. To do this, Google listened first to develop a mixed-use plan that understood community needs and priorities.

For Google, it was essential to build an identity that was authentic to San José's diverse culture. To further amplify the City's creative voice, i collaborated with local photographers and illustrators to help shape the visual language.

With mobile visitors in mind, we designed a website that provided an equally engaging and intuitive experience for users on-the-go.
