Paul Rand always emphasize simplicity in his design because simple color and shape can create powerful designs. For the patterns and color palette, l use yellow circle and blue triangle as the main elements for this project which followed Paul Rand's design concept.
The area of this exhibition area is 120.55m x 78.105m. It contains one entrance and two exits.The whole area is divided into four part, 1920s, 1940s, 1960s and 1980s. Every areas display Paul Rand's artwork in the certain period of time. In addition, there is one Art Shop, one video room and two rest areas, one is a cafe, another one is a snack bar.
