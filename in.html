IN began the business in 2016, having since grown the studio to become one of the most popular in Shanghai. Today, this studio consists of a team of creative professionals who deliver top-notch photography and beatiful tattoos.

The goal is to develop a brand identity and digital presence that immerses customers in elegance while still providing a thorough—and approachable—overview of services and process.

The new website needed to be both luxurious and inviting, so
I incorporated body photography and used skin texture to bring human elements into a minimalist design. We also wanted to reflect the studio’s concept and values by creating a exclusive, welcoming atmosphere through these touches.

In addition, l was able to design a new logo by combining tattoo neddle and pattern of ink.

The goal was to present a clear and effective brand story to educate potential customers visiting online. I partnered with the team at IN tattoo studio to refine the structure of  the website and surface content in an engaging way.

IN provides a seamless viewing experience across all mobile devices, complete with smooth animations and interactions.

By using the neutral accent colors of grey in addition to black and white, we not only signaled tattoo sophistication but also let tattoos speak for themselves.
