This project is about an AI travel assistant called "AVA” which can provides the best travel experience for travelers around the world. The goal is to simplify the scheduling process before travel and organize photos efficiently after travel.

The goal was to present a clear and effective brand story to educate potential customers visiting online.

By thoughtfully surfacing the most relevant content,
i  was able to tell a more intriguing brand story on the homepage.

Delightful colors are combined with sophisticated typefaces and simple icons to keep users engaged while exploring.
